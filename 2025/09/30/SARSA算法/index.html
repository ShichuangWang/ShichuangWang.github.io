回顾折扣回报： \[ U_{t}=R_{t}+\gamma \cdot U_{t+1} \tag{1} \] 假设 \(R_t\) 依赖于 \((S_{t},A_{t},S_{t+1})\),则： \[ \begin{align} \\ Q_{\pi}(s_t,a_t)&=\mathbb{E}[U_t|s_t,a_t] \\ &=\mathbb{E}[R_t+\gamma \cdot U_{t+1}|s_t,a_t] \\ &=\mathbb{E}[R_t|s_t,a_t]+\gamma \cdot \mathbb{E}[U_{t+1}|s_t,a_t] \\ &=\mathbb{E}[R_t|s_t,a_t]+\gamma \cdot \mathbb{E}[Q_{\pi}(S_{t+1},A_{t+1})|s_t,a_t] \\ &=\mathbb{E}[R_{t}+\gamma\cdot Q_{\pi}(S_{t+1},A_NaN)|s_{t},a_{t}] \end{align}\tag{2}\] Tips： \[ Q_{\pi}(S_{t+1},A_{t+1})=\mathbb{E}_{S_{t+2},A_{t+2},\dots,S_{n},A{n}} [U_{t+1}|S_{t+1},A_{t+1}]\tag{3} \] 使用观测值 \(r_t\) 和 \(Q_{\pi}(s_{t+1},a_{t+1})\) 近似估计 \(R_t\) 和 \(Q_{\pi}(S_{t+1},A_{t+1})\),得到TD target： \[ y_{t}=r_{t}+\gamma \cdot Q_{\pi}(s_{t+1},a_{t+1})\tag{4} \] # 表格学习 1. 观察状态转移 \((s_{t},a_{t},r_{t},s_{t+1})\) 2. 根据策略函数 \(\pi(\cdot|s_{t+1})\) 抽样得到动作 \(a_{t+1}\) 3. TD target : \(y_t=r_{t}+\gamma\cdot Q_{\pi}(s_{t+1},a_{t+1})\) 4. TD error : \(\delta_{t}=Q_{\pi}(s_{t},a_{t})-y_{t}\) 5. 更新：\(Q_{\pi}(s_{t},a_{t})\leftarrow \space Q_{\pi}(s_{t},a_{t})-\alpha\cdot \delta_{t}\) # 神经网络学习 1. 使用价值网络 \(q(s,a;w)\) 近似价值函数 \(Q_{\pi}(s,a)\) ，其中 \(w\) 是网络参数 2. TD target ： \(y_{t}=r_{t}+\gamma \cdot q_{s_{t+1},a_{t+1};w}\) 3. TD error : \(\delta_{t}=q(s_{t},a_{t};w)-y_{t}\) 4. Loss : \({\delta_{t}^{2}}\small/{2}\) 5. 求梯度： \(\frac{\partial{\small{\delta_{t}^{2}}\small/{2}}}{\partial w}=\delta_{t}\cdot \frac{\partial \space{q(s_{t},a_{t};w)}}{\partial \space w}\) 6. 梯度下降： \(w \leftarrow \space w-\alpha \cdot \delta_{t}\cdot\frac{\partial \space{q(s_{t},a_{t};w)}}{\partial \space w}\) 7.
